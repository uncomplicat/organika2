    Landscaping

    Is it a garden renovation to lift your outdoor game, a new build to create your dream space or a pre-sale spruce up? Our Organika Landscaping team specializes in hardscaping and softscaping and work with you to create a liveable and purposeful space that gives you pride, joy and peace of mind.

    Hardscaping

    🍃  Timber decks, patios, storage seating
    🍃  Pergolas, entrance canopies, walkway covers
    🍃  Water fountains, ponds, moats, rock drains
    🍃  Retaining walls, rock gardens
    🍃  Fire pits, bbq areas
    🍃  Paving installation, revival
    🍃  Garden beds (raised, tiered, brick/wood)

    Softscaping

    🍃  Trees/plants - natives, ornamentals, edibles
    🍃  Turf /lawns - natural /eco-synthetic
    🍃  Irrigation - build/repair, maintenance
    🍃  Garden maintenance (scheduled & once-off)
    🍃  Pre-sale, pre-event makeovers
    🍃  Custom garden art
